GearUpGarage MVP

Revolutionizing vehicle repair with doorstep services and real-time tracking.

User Perspective

Advantages

  • Saves time with doorstep services.
  • Real-time tracking of the mechanic's arrival.
  • Allows users to speak with the mechanic before they start their journey.
  • Convenient pickup and drop services for users.
  • Visiting nearby recommended centers is an alternative option.
  • Multifunctional toolkits simplify onsite repairs.
  • Saves users from miscommunication with features like photo uploads for problem identification.
  • Transparent pricing and service tracking.

Challenges

  • High service charges compared to local mechanics.
  • Long waiting times for mechanics to arrive.
  • Difficulty in handling major repairs (e.g., engine works) at the doorstep.
  • Possibility of mechanics arriving without required tools or spare parts.
  • Warranty claims and follow-ups with the same mechanic may be challenging.
  • Miscommunication between users and mechanics can lead to misunderstandings.
  • Some services may not be feasible for doorstep execution.
  • Lack of transparency in spare part quality and pricing.
  • Unclear refund or failed repair policies.
  • Unclear scope of services listed on the website.
  • Overcharging concerns if users are unfamiliar with repair costs.

Mechanic Perspective

Advantages

  • Transparent pricing details, including long-distance extra charges, help mechanics prepare for each service.
  • A clear structure on how money is charged (commission-based or service-based) ensures clarity for mechanics.
  • A simple and user-friendly interface for mechanics improves ease of use.
  • Good customer support encourages a smoother experience for mechanics.
  • Ability to view issues beforehand through photos or videos makes it easier to understand and prepare for jobs.
  • Flexibility to take jobs based on their free time or schedule.
  • Fair service charges help mechanics earn appropriately for their effort.
  • Listing all services in detail helps mechanics identify which jobs to take.

Challenges

  • Transporting spare parts or tools to the doorstep is difficult and time-consuming.
  • Public transport for mechanics can lead to delays in reaching service locations.
  • Insufficient workforce may result in higher workloads and delays during peak times.
  • Miscommunication with users who are not educated or tech-savvy can cause confusion about the problems reported.
